Research Overview

Bridging artificial intelligence and cybersecurity to address emerging security challenges in medical AI systems and critical infrastructure.

Mission

As medical AI systems move from research labs to clinical deployment, ensuring their security and robustness becomes critical. Our research focuses on protecting AI models from adversarial attacks, preserving patient privacy, and building AI-powered security tools for threat detection.

Current Focus

Working directly with OAIT's medical AI systems (breast cancer detection, Parkinson's diagnosis, Alzheimer's detection) to implement security measures for clinical deployment. Simultaneously developing computer vision-based security solutions for power line infrastructure monitoring.
